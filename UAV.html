Land-Air UAV

Discover the possibility of UAV system

introduction

This project introduces an innovative small-scale land-air hybrid UAV designed for efficient material transportation. It features a unique conversion mechanism that swiftly transforms flight rotor protectors into ground-traveling wheels. By integrating structural optimization through statics and aerodynamics simulations, the UAV achieves enhanced mobility and endurance. With its adaptability to complex terrains and versatile applications, this UAV represents a breakthrough in transport technology with significant practical value and innovation.

Land-Air Conversion Mechanism

The UAV is equipped with an advanced land-air conversion mechanism that seamlessly transitions between flying and ground-traveling modes. Utilizing a ball screw nut pair and multiple gear sets, the system transforms rotor protectors into functional wheels with precision and efficiency.

A shared motor system powers both flight and ground movement, significantly reducing the overall weight while optimizing energy efficiency and improving endurance. This innovative approach minimizes hardware redundancy and enhances the UAV's operational range.

The mechanism's robust design ensures smooth and reliable transitions, enabling the UAV to adapt effortlessly to diverse terrains and environments. This integration of lightweight construction, dual-functionality, and high performance makes it ideal for versatile, multi-environment applications.
