Project Overview

The BirdNest project aims to transform the way food delivery works by addressing the "last mile" challenge of drone delivery. With traditional methods requiring manual intervention or fixed pickup points, BirdNest introduces a fully automated and contactless delivery system. Designed as a smart "mailbox" for food, BirdNest combines cutting-edge technology and user-focused design to deliver convenience, safety, and efficiency right to your window.

How It Works

BirdNest combines advanced technologies and user-centric design to create a seamless delivery experience. The system is fully automated, ensuring a smooth process from drone arrival to package retrieval without human intervention.
